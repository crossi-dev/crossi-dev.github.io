Velora

AI-native commerce platform with a full MCP server and A2A multi-agent backbone.

Visit site ↗
The problem

Small and mid-size businesses in Argentina run on a tangle of WhatsApp messages, spreadsheets, and manual steps. Every sale, invoice, shipment, and payment requires a person to coordinate across 4–5 disconnected systems. The bottleneck is human coordination — and it doesn't scale.

What I built

A chat-first, AI-native commerce platform where the owner manages the entire business through conversation. The system routes each intent to the right specialized agent, which executes the action end-to-end: register a sale, issue an electronic invoice, dispatch a shipment, collect payment — all from a single chat interface.
